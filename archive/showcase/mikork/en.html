MikoRK pohrebníctvo

Calm, trust-first local brand + website

Overview

Problem: A local funeral services business in Slovakia needed a modern online presence that builds trust, works seamlessly on mobile devices, and respects the sensitive nature of their services. Most inquiries happen on phones, often in difficult circumstances.

Outcome: A calm, dignified, mobile-first website that builds trust immediately, optimizes for local discovery, and includes ethical automation that supports human connection without replacing it.

Deliverables

Brand Refresh
Calm neutral palette, large readable typography, high contrast for accessibility. No aggressive CTAs, no distracting animations. WCAG AA compliant.
Website Template
Mobile-first responsive design. Homepage, contact page, services page, about page. Always-visible phone number, emergency contact block, simple navigation.
Local SEO Basics
Optimized for "pohrebníctvo + mesto" searches. Structured data (LocalBusiness, Organization). Google Maps-ready. Slovak language priority.
Contact Form API
Simple form with email notification to owner. No auto-reply to families (personal response required). GDPR-compliant, privacy-first.
Ethical Automation Rules
Content generator for educational posts (manual approval required). No automated condolences. No automated replies. Automation supports humans, never replaces them.

What We Learned

Respectful design builds trust immediately. Calm, dignified aesthetic matters when families are grieving.

Mobile-first is non-negotiable. Most inquiries happen on phones, often in stressful situations.

Local SEO beats paid ads. Found when families search locally, not through advertising.

Ethical automation saves time without losing human connection. Automation supports humans, never replaces them.

Complete documentation enables independence. Owner can manage website independently with clear guides.
