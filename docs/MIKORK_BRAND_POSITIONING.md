# MikoRK pohrebníctvo - Brand Positioning

**Client:** MikoRK pohrebníctvo  
**Market:** Local (Slovakia / Czech Republic)  
**Sensitivity:** High (death, grief, trust, dignity)  
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-17

---

## Brand Values

### Core Values

1. **Stability**
   - Long-standing presence in the community
   - Reliable, consistent service
   - No sudden changes or disruptions

2. **Care**
   - Genuine compassion
   - Attention to detail
   - Respect for individual needs

3. **Reliability**
   - Always available
   - Clear communication
   - Follow-through on commitments

4. **Human Presence**
   - Personal touch
   - Real people, not automated systems
   - Understanding and empathy

5. **Quiet Professionalism**
   - Dignified service
   - No flashy marketing
   - Respectful approach

---

## Tone of Voice

### What to Say

**✅ Appropriate Language:**

- "Sme tu pre vás" (We are here for you)
- "Poradíme vám" (We will advise you)
- "S úctou a pochopením" (With respect and understanding)
- "Dlhodobá tradícia" (Long-standing tradition)
- "Profesionálna starostlivosť" (Professional care)
- "Váš čas, váš spôsob" (Your time, your way)
- "Diskrétne a dôstojne" (Discreet and dignified)

**✅ Communication Style:**

- Calm and reassuring
- Clear and direct
- Respectful and dignified
- Helpful without being pushy
- Professional but warm

---

### What Never to Say

**❌ Inappropriate Language:**

- "Kúpte teraz" (Buy now)
- "Obmedzená ponuka" (Limited offer)
- "Akcia" (Sale/Promotion)
- "Zľava" (Discount)
- "Ponáhľajte sa" (Hurry up)
- "Posledná šanca" (Last chance)
- "Exkluzívna ponuka" (Exclusive offer)
- Growth-bro language ("scale", "disrupt", "innovate")
- Startup language ("launch", "pivot", "hustle")

**❌ Inappropriate Communication:**

- Urgency or pressure
- Sales tactics
- Hype or excitement
- Playful or casual tone
- Aggressive marketing
- Automated responses to grief

## Do's and Don'ts for Funeral Services Communication

### Do's ✅

**Communication:**
- ✅ Be clear and direct
- ✅ Offer information without pressure
- ✅ Provide multiple ways to contact
- ✅ Respond promptly but not urgently
- ✅ Use respectful, dignified language
- ✅ Acknowledge difficulty of the situation
- ✅ Offer guidance, not sales pitches

**Content:**
- ✅ Educational information
- ✅ Helpful resources
- ✅ Clear service descriptions
- ✅ Transparent pricing (if appropriate)
- ✅ Contact information always visible
- ✅ Emergency contact prominently displayed

**Design:**
- ✅ Calm, professional appearance
- ✅ Easy to read (large fonts)
- ✅ Simple navigation
- ✅ Mobile-friendly
- ✅ Accessible to older users
- ✅ Clear hierarchy

---

### Don'ts ❌

**Communication:**
- ❌ Create urgency or pressure
- ❌ Use sales tactics
- ❌ Automate condolence messages
- ❌ Send marketing emails to grieving families
- ❌ Use playful or casual language
- ❌ Make assumptions about needs
- ❌ Push for immediate decisions

**Content:**
- ❌ Countdown timers
- ❌ "Buy now" buttons
- ❌ Promotional offers
- ❌ Flashy animations
- ❌ Aggressive CTAs
- ❌ Fake testimonials
- ❌ Exaggerated claims

**Design:**
- ❌ Bright, flashy colors
- ❌ Aggressive animations
- ❌ Complex navigation
- ❌ Small fonts
- ❌ Cluttered layouts
- ❌ Pop-ups or interstitials
- ❌ Auto-playing videos

### Good Examples ✅

**Homepage Headline:**
"Sme tu pre vás v najťažších chvíľach. Profesionálna starostlivosť s úctou a pochopením."

## Brand Positioning Statement

**MikoRK pohrebníctvo** je lokálne pohrebníctvo s dlhodobou tradíciou, ktoré poskytuje dôstojnú a profesionálnu starostlivosť v najťažších chvíľach života. Sme tu pre vás s úctou, pochopením a profesionálnym prístupom.

**MikoRK pohrebníctvo** is a local funeral service with a long-standing tradition, providing dignified and professional care in life's most difficult moments. We are here for you with respect, understanding, and a professional approach.

## Visual Identity Guidelines

### Colors

**Primary Colors:**
- Deep neutrals (navy, charcoal, dark gray)
- Muted accents (soft blue, warm gray)
- No bright or flashy colors

**Avoid:**
- Bright reds, oranges, yellows
- Neon colors
- Aggressive color combinations

### Typography

**Fonts:**
- High readability
- Large sizes (minimum 16px)
- Clear hierarchy
- Accessible to older audiences

**Avoid:**
- Decorative fonts
- Small text
- Hard-to-read fonts

### Imagery

**Appropriate:**
- Calm, peaceful scenes
- Professional, dignified photos
- Local community images
- Respectful, tasteful visuals

**Avoid:**
- Aggressive or flashy images
- Playful or casual photos
- Overly dramatic imagery
- Stock photos that feel fake
