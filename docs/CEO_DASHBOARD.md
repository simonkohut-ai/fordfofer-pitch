### High-intent people count

  - Expressed clear need/pain point

### Conversations in progress
- **Source:** Active DM threads, email threads, scheduled calls
- **Current:** [update manually]
- **Definition:** Any back-and-forth where:
  - You've exchanged at least 2 messages
  - They've shown genuine interest (not just "thanks")
  - There's a potential next step (waitlist, pilot, feedback)

## What we don't track

- Page views
- Social media likes/follows
- GitHub stars
- Newsletter opens (unless they lead to conversations)
- Any metric that doesn't map to a named person

---

## Weekly review questions

1. Did we talk to real people this week?
2. Did anticipation/tension increase?
3. Did anything distract us from `/prelaunch`?
4. How many high-intent people did we add?
5. Are we closer to first revenue?
