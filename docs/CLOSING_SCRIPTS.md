## Qualification Scripts

### Qualification Questions

**1. Intent Check:**
```
What's your biggest marketing challenge right now?
```

**2. Budget Check:**
```
What's your monthly marketing budget? (No judgment, just helps me recommend the right solution)
```

**3. Timeline Check:**
```
When do you need this? (Are you looking to solve this now or later?)
```

**4. Authority Check:**
```
Are you the decision maker, or do you need to check with someone?
```

**5. Pain Point:**
```
What's taking up most of your time with marketing right now?
```

---

### Qualification Logic

**High Intent Signals:**
- ‚úÖ Asks specific questions
- ‚úÖ Mentions budget/timeline
- ‚úÖ Requests demo
- ‚úÖ Already using similar tools
- ‚úÖ Responds quickly
- ‚úÖ Engages with content

**Low Intent Signals:**
- ‚ùå Generic questions
- ‚ùå "Just browsing"
- ‚ùå No budget mentioned
- ‚ùå Not decision maker
- ‚ùå Slow/no response
- ‚ùå Vague needs

**Auto-Qualification:**
- **High Intent:** Move to demo/closing
- **Medium Intent:** Nurture sequence
- **Low Intent:** Disqualify or long-term nurture

## Best Practices

### Do's

‚úÖ **Personalize:** Use their name, mention their company/role
‚úÖ **Add Value:** Share insights, not just promotion
‚úÖ **Be Helpful:** Answer questions, offer demos
‚úÖ **Create Urgency:** Limited spots, time-sensitive offers
‚úÖ **Follow Up:** Most sales happen on 3rd-5th contact
‚úÖ **Track:** Keep notes on what works

### Don'ts

‚ùå **Don't Spam:** Respect their time
‚ùå **Don't Be Pushy:** Be helpful, not aggressive
‚ùå **Don't Ignore Signals:** If they say no, respect it
‚ùå **Don't Over-Promise:** Be honest about what you can deliver
‚ùå **Don't Skip Follow-Ups:** Consistency matters
