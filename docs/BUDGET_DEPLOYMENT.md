# Budget Deployment System

**Goal:** Deploy budget only after revenue is proven  
**Rule:** No paid spend until revenue exists  
**Focus:** Revenue → Budget → Growth loop

---

## Budget Deployment Rules

### Rule 1: Revenue First

**No budget until:**
- [ ] First revenue received (€149+)
- [ ] Revenue source validated
- [ ] Break-even proven

**After first revenue:**
- [ ] Calculate reinvestment amount
- [ ] Deploy budget strategically
- [ ] Track ROI

---

### Rule 2: 40/40/20 Allocation

**For every €1 of net revenue:**

**40% – Growth Budget**
- Email tooling
- Analytics
- Small automations
- Content amplification
- Paid ads (after validation)

**40% – Runway / Buffer**
- Hosting
- Domains
- Personal burn
- Emergency fund

**20% – Tools / Infrastructure**
- Better observability
- Design assets
- Testing tools
- One-time purchases

---

## ROI Measurement

### Growth Budget ROI

**Metrics:**
- Revenue generated from growth spend
- Customers acquired from growth spend
- CAC from growth spend

**Target:**
- ROI > 3x (€1 spent → €3+ revenue)
- CAC < €50 per customer

**If Failed:**
- Cut non-performing channels
- Optimize spend
- Test new channels

---

### Tools Budget ROI

**Metrics:**
- Time saved from tools
- Efficiency gains
- Revenue impact

**Target:**
- Time saved > cost
- Efficiency gains measurable
- Positive revenue impact

**If Failed:**
- Cancel unused tools
- Optimize tool usage
- Test alternatives

## Budget Deployment Checklist

### Before First Revenue

- [ ] No paid spend
- [ ] Use free tools only
- [ ] Focus on organic acquisition
- [ ] Track all costs (time only)

---

### After First Revenue

- [ ] Calculate net revenue
- [ ] Allocate 40/40/20
- [ ] Deploy growth budget strategically
- [ ] Track ROI

---

### Ongoing

- [ ] Track revenue daily
- [ ] Calculate allocations weekly
- [ ] Review ROI monthly
- [ ] Adjust allocations based on performance

---

## Budget Cut Criteria

### Cut If:

**Growth Budget:**
- ROI < 2x after 1 month
- CAC > Revenue per customer
- No conversions after €100+ spend

**Tools Budget:**
- Tool unused after 1 month
- No efficiency gains
- Cost > value

**Runway Budget:**
- Not needed (emergency only)
- Can be reduced if growth budget needs more

## Budget Deployment Examples
