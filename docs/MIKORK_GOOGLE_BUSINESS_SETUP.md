# MikoRK pohrebníctvo - Google Business Profile Setup Guide

**Purpose:** Step-by-step guide for setting up Google Business Profile  
**Audience:** Business owner (non-technical)  
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-17

---

## Why Google Business Profile?

Google Business Profile helps people find your business when they search for:
- "pohrebníctvo [mesto]"
- "pohrebné služby [región]"
- "pohrebná služba [mesto]"

It shows your business information, hours, contact details, and reviews directly in Google search results.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Profile

### Go to Google Business Profile

1. Visit: https://www.google.com/business/
2. Click "Manage now" or "Start now"
3. Sign in with your Google account (or create one)

### Enter Business Information

**Business Name:**
```
MikoRK pohrebníctvo
```

**Category:**
- Search for: "Funeral Home"
- Select: "Funeral Home" or "Pohrebníctvo" (if available in Slovak)

**Address:**
- Enter your complete business address
- Make sure it matches your website exactly

**Service Area (if applicable):**
- If you serve multiple cities, add them here
- Example: "Bratislava, Trnava, Nitra"

**Phone Number:**
```
+421 XXX XXX XXX
```
(Make sure this matches your website)

**Website:**
```
https://www.mikork.sk
```
(Update with your actual domain)

---

## Step 2: Verify Your Business

Google will ask you to verify your business. Choose one:

**Option 1: Postcard (Recommended)**
- Google sends a postcard to your business address
- Takes 5-7 business days
- Enter verification code when received

**Option 2: Phone**
- Google calls your business phone
- Enter verification code immediately

**Option 3: Email**
- Google sends email to your business email
- Check email and enter code

**Important:** Complete verification before moving to next steps.

---

## Step 3: Complete Your Profile

### Business Hours

**If 24/7:**
- Select "Open 24 hours"
- Apply to all days

**If specific hours:**
- Enter your actual hours
- Example: "Mo-Fr 08:00-17:00, Sa 09:00-13:00, Su closed"

### Business Description

**Use this text (or customize):**

```
MikoRK pohrebníctvo poskytuje dôstojnú a profesionálnu starostlivosť v najťažších chvíľach života. Sme tu pre vás s úctou, pochopením a profesionálnym prístupom. Lokálna spoločnosť s dlhodobou tradíciou.

K dispozícii 24/7. Kontaktujte nás telefónom alebo emailom.
```

**Keep it:**
- ✅ Professional and respectful
- ✅ Clear and helpful
- ✅ Under 750 characters

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Sales language
- ❌ Urgency or pressure
- ❌ "Buy now" or "limited offer"

---

## Step 4: Add Photos

### What Photos to Upload

**✅ Good Photos:**
- Exterior of your business (if applicable)
- Professional, calm images
- Clear, well-lit photos
- Respectful and dignified

**❌ Avoid:**
- Personal photos
- Unprofessional images
- Photos that feel salesy
- Low-quality images

### How Many Photos

- **Minimum:** 3-5 photos
- **Recommended:** 10-15 photos
- **Update:** Add new photos monthly (optional)

---

## Step 5: Posts (Optional but Recommended)

### What to Post

**✅ Appropriate Posts:**

**Informational:**
```
Dušičky sú dňom, keď si pripomíname našich blízkych. Sme tu pre vás, ak potrebujete podporu alebo informácie.
```

**Educational:**
```
Čo všetko zahŕňa organizácia pohrebu? Kontaktujte nás, poradíme vám a odpovieme na všetky vaše otázky.
```

**Seasonal:**
```
V tomto období si pripomíname význam rodiny a komunity. Sme tu pre vás, ak potrebujete pomoc.
```

**❌ What NOT to Post:**

- Sales language ("Kúpte teraz", "Obmedzená ponuka")
- Urgency or pressure ("Ponáhľajte sa")
- Promotional offers ("Zľava 20%")
- Aggressive marketing
- Playful or casual content

### How Often to Post

- **Frequency:** 1-2 posts per month
- **Quality over quantity:** Better to post less with good content
- **Timing:** Post during business hours (if applicable)

### How to Post

1. Go to your Google Business Profile
2. Click "Posts" in the menu
3. Click "Create post"
4. Choose post type (usually "What's new")
5. Write your post (use examples above)
6. Add photo (optional but recommended)
7. Click "Publish"

---

## Step 6: Reviews

### Enable Reviews

- Reviews are automatically enabled
- You cannot disable them
- This is good - reviews build trust

### How to Respond to Reviews

**✅ Good Response:**
```
Ďakujeme za vašu spätnú väzbu. Sme radi, že sme vám mohli pomôcť v tejto ťažkej dobe.
```

**Keep responses:**
- Professional and respectful
- Personal (not template)
- Grateful and empathetic

**Avoid:**
- Generic responses
- Defensive language
- Sales language

---

## Step 7: Keep Your Profile Updated

### Regular Updates

**Monthly:**
- Check that all information is correct
- Update hours if they change
- Add new photos (optional)
- Post 1-2 times (optional)

**When Changes Occur:**
- Update address immediately
- Update phone number immediately
- Update hours immediately
- Update website if it changes

## Common Questions

### Q: Do I need to post every week?

**A:** No. 1-2 posts per month is enough. Quality over quantity.

### Q: What if I get a bad review?

**A:** Respond professionally and respectfully. Offer to discuss privately. Never be defensive.

### Q: Can I use the same content on Facebook?

**A:** Yes, but customize it slightly. Don't copy-paste exactly.

### Q: How do I know if it's working?

**A:** Check your Google Business Profile insights. Look for:
- How many people viewed your profile
- How many people called
- How many people visited your website
