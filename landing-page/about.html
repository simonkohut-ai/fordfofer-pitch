About Chiara's World

Founder: Goliáš Čapovič

Goliáš Čapovič is the Founder and CEO of Chiara's World. He created the revolutionary P2BA (Prompt-to-Business Automation) Platform - an autonomous AI system that transforms natural language commands into fully functional businesses.

Our Mission

Chiara's World was created to make AI trading and business automation accessible, transparent, and trustworthy. We believe in building a platform where everyone feels welcome and informed.

About the Project

Chiara's World is an AI Trading Platform and P2BA system that combines cutting-edge technology with a warm, approachable design. Launched in 2024 by Goliáš Čapovič, we're committed to transparency, user trust, and autonomous business creation.

P2BA Platform

The P2BA (Prompt-to-Business Automation) Platform is our flagship innovation. It allows users to create complete businesses - from dropshipping stores to influencer marketing campaigns - simply by typing a command. The system uses specialized AI agents to handle code generation, marketing strategy, and external integrations automatically.
