# Pre-Launch Strategy - Daily Execution Plan

**Goal:** Generate revenue BEFORE 21.12.2025

---

## Daily Actions (30 minutes/day)

### Morning (10 min)

1. **Post Teaser** (Social Media)
   - Copy: Locked teaser (Golo Čapo / PTK – Někdo půjde z kola ven / 21.12)
   - Bio link: `/prelaunch`
   - No CTA in post (builds curiosity)

2. **Check Growth Panel**
   - Open `/dashboard` → Automation Hub
   - Check: Total Leads, Customers, Conversion Rate
   - Note any new leads from yesterday

### Afternoon (10 min)

3. **Manual DM Outreach** (5-10 messages)
   - Use `/social-kit` for templates
   - Target: Founders, creators, marketers
   - Goal: Drive to `/prelaunch`

4. **Share Link**
   - Stories: Link to `/prelaunch`
   - Comments: "Check bio for early access"
   - DMs: Direct link to `/prelaunch`

### Evening (10 min)

5. **Monitor Metrics**
   - Check War Room (`/war-room`)
   - Review conversion funnel
   - Track revenue (€49 per customer)

---

## Weekly Actions (1 hour/week)

### Monday: Review & Plan

- Review last week's metrics
- Check survey responses (`/survey` submissions)
- Plan DM outreach targets
- Update social kit templates if needed

### Wednesday: Test Systems

- Test email automations
- Verify Stripe webhook
- Check `/prelaunch` page
- Test `/pricing` redirect

### Friday: Analyze

- Review conversion rate
- Identify top lead sources
- Plan next week's strategy
